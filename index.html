Fanding Huang (黄仁勋)

I am a first-year master student at Shenzhen International Graduate School, Tsinghua University. Before that, I got B.S. in Automation from Hangzhou Dianzi University (HDU) in 2024.

My previous research interests primarily lie in Computer Vision, Domain Adaptation and GNN. My current research focuses on Test-time Adaptation, MLLM & LLMs.
